About Kannan Photography

Kannan's photography involves capturing images at various events such as weddings, concerts, parties, conferences, and more. The goal of our photography is to document the event, its atmosphere, and the people involved in a visually appealing and meaningful way. The end result of our photography can be a treasured keepsake for those involved in the event, providing memories that will last a lifetime.

Wedding photography is the art of capturing the special moments and emotions of a wedding day. It is an important aspect of a wedding as it captures memories that last a lifetime.

  • A good wedding photographer will prepare thoroughly, researching the venue and couple's preferences to ensure they capture the best images.
  • A photographer should bring creativity and imagination to the table, capturing candid moments as well as traditional posed shots in a unique and artistic way.
  • A photographer should be reliable, punctual and professional in their approach to the wedding day.
  • A photographer should have good communication skills, working closely with the couple and other vendors to ensure the photography runs smoothly.
  • The photographer should pay attention to every detail, from capturing the rings to the decor, to create a comprehensive and beautiful album of the wedding day.

Newborn photography is the art of capturing the beauty and innocence of a newborn baby.when they are most sleepy and pliable, allowing for a range of poses and setups.

  • Newborns can be unpredictable, so a photographer must have patience and be able to work around feeding, sleeping, and crying schedules.
  • Newborn photography should be creative, capturing the unique beauty of each baby and producing beautiful, one-of-a-kind images that parents will treasure forever.
  • A photographer should pay attention to the details, such as the baby's positioning, lighting, and background, to create stunning images that truly stand out.
  • Good lighting, composition, and post-production skills are essential for producing high-quality newborn photographs.
  • The photographer should work closely with the parents to understand their vision for the shoot and incorporate their preferences into the final images.

Maternity photography is the art of capturing the beauty and excitement of a woman's pregnancy, her beauty and excitement for the arrival of her baby.

  • Maternity photography should focus on the mother-to-be and showcase her beauty, strength, and excitement for her new role as a parent.
  • Maternity photography should be creative, capturing unique poses and compositions that highlight the mom-to-be and her baby bump.
  • A photographer should ensure that the mom-to-be is comfortable throughout the shoot, providing supportive props and posing that flatter her and make her feel at ease.
  • Good lighting, composition, and post-production skills are essential for producing high-quality maternity photographs.
  • The photographer should work closely with the mom-to-be to understand her vision for the shoot and incorporate her preferences into the final images.

Birthday party photography is the art of capturing the fun, excitement, and memories of a birthday celebration. Capturing the special moments and atmosphere of the party.

  • A good birthday party photographer will prepare for the event by researching the venue, getting a sense of the birthday person's style, and ensuring they have the necessary equipment for the job.
  • A birthday party photographer should capture candid shots of the guests having fun, dancing, and enjoying the celebration.
  • The photographer should pay attention to the details of the event, from decorations to balloons, to create a comprehensive and beautiful album of the birthday celebration.
  • A photographer should bring creativity and imagination to the table, capturing the party in a unique and artistic way.
  • Good lighting, composition, and post-production skills are essential for producing high-quality birthday party photographs.
